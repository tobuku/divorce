Earth

Earth is stability. It covers the practical moves that reduce chaos and create room to breathe. Start with basics, then stack improvements.

Practical support
  • Professional organizers, paper and home reset
  • Moving and storage coordination, downsizing help
  • Meal prep services or grocery delivery support
  • House cleaning and laundry pickup services
  • Childcare support, drop in care, vetted sitters, after school help
  • Routine builder coach, daily structure, sleep schedule, daily plan
  • Trauma informed bodywork, massage, acupuncture, gentle somatic support
  • Budget coaches and financial coaches, bill triage, budgeting
  • Credit counseling, credit repair, debt management support
  • Housing transition support, room rental search help, relocation resources

Fast start

  • Pick one room. Reset it.
  • List top five bills. Set dates.
  • Set a simple morning routine.
  • Lock in one childcare backup option.

What to collect

  • Your monthly fixed costs
  • Your current living setup and constraints
  • Your available time blocks each week
  • Your top three stress triggers at home
