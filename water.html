Water

Water is emotional support. It helps you regulate, grieve, and rebuild safety. Choose support that matches your nervous system today.

Emotional support
  • Divorce support groups, online and local
  • Individual therapy, grief, betrayal, trauma informed
  • EMDR or somatic therapy providers
  • Divorce coach focused on emotional regulation
  • Guided journaling circles or women’s circles
  • Meditation and mindfulness instructors, divorce focused
  • Breathwork facilitators, gentle, nervous system focused
  • Sound baths or restorative yoga practitioners
  • Spiritual care options, chaplain, faith based support groups
  • Crisis support resources, hotlines, safety planning services

Fast start

  • Pick one support group. Attend once.
  • Book one therapy intake.
  • Create a short grounding plan.
  • Write a safety plan if risk exists.

What to ask providers

  • Trauma training and approach
  • Session structure and pacing
  • Homework style and between session support
  • Sliding scale or insurance options
