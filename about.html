How to use this

Pick the bucket that matches your current state, not your ideal plan. Then choose one service type and one action.

Rule one

Start with Earth when life feels unstable. Food, sleep, bills, and basic logistics.

Rule two

Start with Water when emotion is flooding. Safety, regulation, and connection.

Rule three

Start with Air when decisions are stuck. Planning, scripts, and systems.

Rule four

Start with Fire when you need momentum. Boundaries, strength, and re entry.
